Scene 01 — The White Background Hero

Master Prompt

Close-up product hero shot of a single glossy pastel powder-blue pebble-shaped jar, smooth
two-lobed ceramic form with a softly domed lid, centered on a seamless white-to-pale-grey
backdrop with no props. The only legible text is a small clean "FrameGen" wordmark in thin
coral-red lettering, no other text anywhere in frame. Shot on a 100mm macro lens at f/9, slight
three-quarter angle revealing the jar's rounded dimensionality, full sharpness edge to edge. Lit
with flat, soft, even front-diffused studio light from a large overhead softbox, neutral 5500K white
balance, one soft specular highlight rolling across the glossy dome, faint contact shadow beneath.
