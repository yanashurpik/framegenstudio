/ 01Manifesto
Why work with me
⌗

Most agencies shoot one ad, hope it works, then ghost. I ship 8 to 30 variants a week — same brand, different hooks — and let the algorithm pick the winner.

No casting, no lighting trucks, no two-week edits. Just a tight research loop, a brand DNA file, and a stack of generative tools that turn a script into a scroll-stopping 15-second spot before the coffee gets cold.

Four moves
from brief to bidder.

Every project follows the same loop. Research first, model last — that's how an AI ad ends up feeling like it was filmed on someone's iPhone instead of generated by a robot at 3am.

/ 01

Mine the winners.

I pull live spend data from the Meta & TikTok ad libraries through dedicated competitor-intel tools, then strip out everything except what's actually being scaled by paid teams in your niche.

/ 02

Decode the pattern.

Every winning ad gets timestamped: hook in the first 1.3s, problem at 4s, payoff at 9s, CTA at 13s. I tag formats, framings, captions and pacing so we know exactly which levers move the needle.

/ 03

Build a brand DNA.

One document that locks the visual + verbal world of your brand — characters, palette, lighting, vocabulary, props, edit rhythm. It's the source of truth every model gets prompted against, so 30 variants still feel like one campaign.
